Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most expensive thing about a bathroom remodel?

Modifying your bathroom layout can be a costly part of a remodel due to the necessary demolition, removal, and disposal of existing fixtures, and any alterations involving drywall cutting will necessitate drywall replacement, contributing to the overall expenses.

Why remodeling a bathroom is expensive?

Bathroom remodeling is typically one of the most expensive home projects, as it often entails skilled labor, plumbing and electrical work, the use of high-end materials and fixtures, and potential updates to the room's layout.

Is DIY bathroom remodel worth it?

In summary, a bathroom remodel will typically cost less than half of what a general contractor (GC) would charge, as it allows you to keep the material costs while saving on labor expenses, but the decision to hire a GC may be justified for the convenience it offers, which can be considered a luxury worth the additional cost.

Do you paint first or last in bathroom remodel?

During kitchen or bathroom remodeling projects, painting is typically one of the final tasks to be completed, as the removal of various items during these renovations poses a risk to painted surfaces, and any wall damage that occurs can be addressed after the remodeling work is finished.
